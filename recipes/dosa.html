Dosa Recipe

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. It is crispy, light, and usually served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for cooking)

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds thoroughly.
  2. Soak them in water for 6â€“8 hours.
  3. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter.
  4. Add salt and mix well.
  5. Allow the batter to ferment overnight.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa.
  7. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in a circular motion.
  8. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  9. Fold and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Serve dosa hot with coconut chutney, tomato chutney, and sambar.

Enjoy your homemade dosa!
